Spark Hospitals forays into home care services, ensuring high quality healthcare becomes more accessible to the community. Integrated healthcare for elderly, chronically ill patients and patients recovering post surgery guarantees that they will now receive personalized attention from well - trained and qualified healthcare providers at their homes.
Spark HomeCare is an extension of Spark Hospitals' care continuum and excellence in healthcare provision. Care will be administered through plan-based customizable programs that incorporate partnership between family members and the care givers. Spark HomeCare is a viable option for long term illness or disease management.
