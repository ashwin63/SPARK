Patient care and comfort



Great pride is taken in our tradition of high standards of patient care. Our aim is to
provide a caring environment which will promote a speedy and comfortable recovery. You
will be cared for by our team of staff who are highly qualified and dedicate.This 
includes your consultantsupported by a range of other healthcare professionals to
ensure your stay is as comfortable as possible.


A whole range of services including, but not limited to, a varied menu selection, flexible 
visiting hours, privacy or services for your family and visitors will contribute to a sense
of warmth and comfort during your stay. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to ask, 
there is always someone on hand to give advice.
